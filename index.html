About Us

Halellujah!!!!! Welcome to Celestial Church of Christ Mount Horeb parish
We are a vibrant community with a desire to serve God
with the whole of our hearts. We are dedicated to the overall growth
of our members and will love for you to join us. Come and be blessed!.
Celestial Church of Christ is a prophetic church with a Christian background.
The faithful are called Celestians, and the church is sometimes informally called “Cele”
The formal name of the church is inspired by a vision by which Jesus would
have said that Church members adore him as do the angels in heaven The name of the
church comes from Deuteronomy 26:15: "Look down from thy Holy habitation, from heaven,
and bless thy people Israel and the land which thou hast given us, as thou didst
swear to our father, a land flowing with milk and honey".
